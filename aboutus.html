Dining in Boston

Boston is filled with a variety of restaurants and other food establishments to help quench anyone's appetite. From the amazing Italian food of the North End, to the bars and grills in Fenway, to the Asian cuisines scattered throughout the city, there is no shortage of dining destinations to quench one's desires. To learn more about eating in Boston, feel free to visit the Food page.

Traveling in Boston

Founded in 1630, Boston is one of the oldest cities in America. Given its age, many of the most noteworthy tourist attractions pertain to historical sites pertaining to the founding of the United States. However, there exists a diverse group of historical and sensational places to visit appealing to a variety of tastes and opions. For sports fans, Fenway Park represents a symbol of America's pastime. For nature lovers, the Boston Public Garden and the Boston Common are two must-sees. For history nerds, the Freedom Trail includes a number of bygone attractions. To learn more about where to travel in Boston, feel free to visit the Travel page.
